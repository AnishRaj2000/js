Personas give your account a face. Because of this, your personas are public and stored in plaintext in the database, allowing other users to find you and share with you (and also allowing you to share with others). The only required information for a persona is the screen name, which must be unique.

While personas themselves are public, the link between a persona and your account is hidden and encrypted.
